Dear Future_me,

I'm writing this letter to you at the age of 20. I think a lot like will I be successful or not? Is the path I'm taking in life right? What I'm doing right?

There are so many problems that I want to end and I can only cry and talk to him that he will take care of me and I hope he will guide me in my life plan. Even though I don't always talk to him and with so many sins in my life, I hope he will still guide me and especially my parents and my loved ones, and I hope one day I can fulfill my dreams in life.

I just want to tell you that I will never get up from the struggles that I'm facing right now. Because life is like a road trip, you never know your destination until you're there. You never know when there is traffic that waits a short or long time if you're going to your destination.

There's going to be a turn right or left in life. I have mixed problems and academic pressure to the point that I thought about ending my life, but I didn't do that because I wanted to see you and spend my life with you. And life is not easy for me because I have an overthinking mind and a sensitive heart. So, I just enjoy, cry, go with the flow, and pass on those problems in what I am doing for myself and you, my future.

Let this letter promise you that I will be the best kind of person I strive to be and I can be for you.

Nhathalyn Sacamil
